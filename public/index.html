About Artwork Hunt

Artwork Hunt is a community of art lovers, where artists post their drawings or paintings to be discovered. We making it easy to discover new artists around the world & to share your creations. One of the challenges for artists is gaining publicity and attracting attention from potential collectors, we provide a platform for solving that problem.

Art Lovers

Our platform makes it easy for you to discover some of the best artwork around the world. You can upvote on your favorite artwork, follow your favorite artists and curate your favorite artwork.

Artists

It's all about the artist, Get the exposure your artwork needs. Our platform makes it easy for your work to be discovered rather than being buried in the constant noise in all the art space that we've become customed to.

Community

Our community is about the artwork and your artwork is the product. The concept and the story that comes with your artwork grab the attention of the audience - We bring the audience. Artwork that are posted by the artists are ranked and featured on the homepage of Artwork Hunt for a period of time, based on the engagement, the upvotes by the community and other factors. Every artwork that makes it to the homepage will ultimately promote the owner of the artwork for that period.
